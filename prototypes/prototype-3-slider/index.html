Building a Capture Habit

Building a capture habit requires making it frictionless. Here's a 3-step approach:

1. Choose one ubiquitous tool - Use something always with you (phone app, small notebook).

2. Create a trigger - Capture every time you feel mental friction or think "I should remember this."

3. Process daily for 21 days - Research shows habits form through consistent repetition. Set a daily alarm to review your capture system.
